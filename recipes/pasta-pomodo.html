Pasta pomodoro

Recipe description

The pasta pomodo is a well know italian recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500 g of spaghetti
  • 5 liters of water
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Put the water in a saucepan and boil it
  2. Add the spaghetti and wait 10 minutes
  3. Slice the garlic and the onion
  4. Put the olive oil, the garlic and the onion in a pan
  5. When they are transparent, add the tomato sauce
  6. Drain the water of the spaghetti
  7. Put the spaghetti in the pan with the garlic, onion and tomato sauce
  8. Serve it !
