Soja Rice

Recipe description

The soja rice is a simple yet delicious and cheap recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500 g of rice
  • 5 liters of water
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic
  • Soja sauce

Steps

  1. Put the water in a saucepan and boil it
  2. Add the rice and wait 10 minutes
  3. Slice the garlic and the onion
  4. Put the olive oil, the garlic and the onion in a pan
  5. When they are transparent, add the soja sauce
  6. Drain the water of the rice
  7. Put the rice in the pan with the garlic, onion and soja sauce
  8. Serve it !
