Spanish Beans

Recipe description

The spanish beans is a well equilibrated meal for everyone.

It containes meat, vegetables, cereals, a bit of fat and plenty of love ;-).

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of red beans
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 big spoon of olive oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the carrots and put it in the saucepan with water to boil
  2. Slice the onion
  3. Put the olive oil in a pan
  4. Add the onion
  5. Cut the chorizo
  6. When the onion in transparent, add the chorizo
  7. When the carrots are ready (you know they are ready when you can put a knife easily in it), add them to the onion and chorizo
  8. Let them marinate for 5 minutes
  9. Add the can of red beans and let them marinate for 10 minutes
  10. Add the tomato sauce and let all the ingredients marinate for an hour
  11. Serve it hot and enjoy !
